The Price of a Black Lotus is
$166,100

The Black Lotus is the most coveted card in all of Magic the Gathering. In a game full of dragons and wizards, a simple flower reigns supreme. The Black Lotus is so powerful, it was ultimately restricted or outright banned in most tournaments.


On February 27, 2019 a Gem Mint BGS 9.5 Black Lotus sold on eBay for $166,100. While all Black Loti are rare, the price can vary greatly by print and grading. This site is dedicated to tracking the price across various auctions and compiling them into a single source. As always, past prices may not hold in the future.
